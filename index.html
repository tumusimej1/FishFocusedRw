We are passionate fishing enthusiasts dedicated to promoting sustainable fishing practices in Rwanda. Buying Tips

  • Check for clear, bright eyes and red gills
  • Press the flesh - should be firm and spring back
  • Best prices early morning
  • Negotiate prices for bulk purchases
