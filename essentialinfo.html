Fishing Regulations & Guidelines in Rwanda

Fishing Laws in Rwanda

Fishing in Rwanda is regulated to ensure sustainable practices and protect aquatic ecosystems. The following laws apply:

  • All fishermen must obtain a valid fishing permit from the Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB).
  • Fishing is prohibited in protected areas and during breeding seasons.
  • Use of illegal fishing methods (e.g., explosives, poison) is strictly prohibited.

Sustainability Guidelines

Rwanda promotes sustainable fishing practices to protect its water bodies. Key guidelines include:

  • Use of approved fishing gear to minimize environmental impact.
  • Respect for catch limits to prevent overfishing.
  • Reporting illegal fishing activities to authorities.
